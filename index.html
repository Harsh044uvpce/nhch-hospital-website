WHO WE ARE

A Step Towards Next-Gen Healthcare

New Health City Hospital & Research Center is central India's premier destination for pediatric and critical care. We combine cutting-edge technology with compassionate expertise to deliver outstanding patient outcomes.

Located in the heart of Nagpur, our facility features a Level III NICU, Pediatric ICU, advanced diagnostic imaging, and a dedicated research wing committed to advancing medical science.

WHAT WE OFFER

Our Medical Specialities

Neonatology & NICU

Level III NICU with latest ventilators and monitoring systems for premature and critically ill newborns.

Pediatric Cardiology

Comprehensive diagnosis of congenital heart diseases in children with advanced echocardiography.

Pediatric Neurology

Expert care for childhood epilepsy and neurological conditions using cutting-edge diagnostics.

Pediatric Pulmonology

Specialized treatment for asthma, pneumonia, and chronic lung conditions in children.

Diagnostic Imaging

Digital X-ray, ultrasound, CT scan, and MRI with pediatric protocols and rapid reporting.

24/7 Emergency

Round-the-clock emergency care with dedicated pediatric emergency physicians always on call.
